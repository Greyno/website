Our main meeting is held once a month, usually on the second Thursday of the month. The meeting starts at 6:00pm with a half hour of social pizza time. After some introductions and group news we breaks into presentations. These presentations can be anywhere from 10-15m to more formalized talks (~30m each). The meeting ends around 8:30pm but some people usually followup the meeting at a local bar for a drink. All are welcome to join us.

If you have any questions or would like to present at the meeting, please get in touch with Mike Zornek, zorn@phillycocoa.org.
